The most potent way in which I can help you is through one-on-one ayurvedic consultations. We use all things ayurveda — diet, lifestyle, herbs, self care, movement, yoga, subtle energy therapies, and spiritual counseling to correct imbalances and establish health and true wellbeing. This method is rooted in the ancient but translated for the modern so that it’s practical, helpful, and actually feels good to implement.
